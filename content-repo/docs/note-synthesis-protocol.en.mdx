## Two-Step Spin-Coating Protocol for CsPbI3

### Materials

- CsI (99.999%, Sigma-Aldrich)
- PbI2 (99.99%, TCI)
- DMF (anhydrous, Sigma-Aldrich)
- DMSO (anhydrous, Sigma-Aldrich)

### Step 1: PbI2 Film Deposition

1. Dissolve PbI2 in DMF:DMSO (9:1 v/v) at 1.0 M concentration
2. Stir at 70°C for 2 hours until fully dissolved
3. Spin-coat at 3000 rpm for 30 seconds onto cleaned ITO substrate
4. Anneal at 70°C for 1 minute

### Step 2: CsI Treatment

1. Prepare 1.5 mg/mL CsI solution in isopropanol
2. Spin-coat onto PbI2 film at 2000 rpm for 30 seconds
3. Anneal at 150°C for 10 minutes in nitrogen atmosphere

### Common Failure Modes

- **Incomplete conversion**: CsI concentration too low or annealing temperature insufficient
- **Pinholes**: Spin speed too high or substrate not properly cleaned
- **Phase instability**: Ambient humidity above 30% during annealing

### Tips

The [[mat-perovskite-abx3|perovskite]] phase is sensitive to moisture. Always work in a glovebox or dry room with RH < 20%.
