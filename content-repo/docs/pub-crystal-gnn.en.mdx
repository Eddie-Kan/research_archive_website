## Abstract

Predicting the thermodynamic stability of perovskite crystals is essential for accelerating materials discovery. We introduce CrystalGNN, a graph neural network that encodes periodic crystal structures as multi-edge graphs with learnable angular features.

## Method

CrystalGNN represents each crystal as a graph where:
- **Nodes** correspond to atoms, featurized by element embeddings
- **Edges** represent bonds within a cutoff radius, with distance and angular features
- **Message passing** aggregates neighbor information over 3 layers

The model outputs per-atom energies that are summed and normalized to predict formation energy per atom.
